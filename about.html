About us

Of all the people involved in the creation of this site, none are foreign to the world of immigration. Our collaborators are specialists and experts from a wide range of fields, including immigration and citizenship law, accountants, brokers, real estate agents, sworn translators and much more. We are well equipped and staffed in order to help you in any and all capacities in your citizenship or immigration needs.

Inimex Inc. is specialized in providing individuals representation and guidance in various areas of immigration and citizenship by investment programs. We provide tailored solutions and services to business people, investors and entrepreneurs wishing to relocate and develop their business abroad.

Our mission is to provide individuals with the proper means to obtain a citizenship or residency by investment while guiding one step at a time towards the full realization of the project. We take care of the files with the utmost care and privacy, help individuals choose the most suitable program, translate documents with precision and are always present to answer your questions.

Having collaborators and partners in each country for many years, we decided to unite our teams together to help individuals navigate through complex markets to make the process as smooth as possible.

Our team speaks various languages, including English, French, German, Russian, Spanish and Chinese.
